Official Agreement Overview

Duro Colors Private Limited was established in 2007 and has been manufacturing high-quality paints, primers, putty, cement-based paints, tile grout, adhesive, and epoxy coatings.

Since April 25, 2018, Duro Colors Private Limited has been in technical collaboration with Regent Paints Inc., USA, headquartered at 69-44 Cooper Avenue, Glendale, New York, 11385, USA.

Regent Paints Inc., USA is a leading manufacturer and exporter of decorative and industrial paints, along with related items.
