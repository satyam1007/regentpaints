Regent Paints Partnership

Official Agreement Overview

📅 Date: April 25, 2018

🏢 Company: Regent Paints Inc, USA

👤 Authorized Party: Duro Colors Pvt Ltd

🌍 Operational Region: Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan (India)

Duro Colors Private Limited was established in 2007 and has been manufacturing high-quality paints, primers, putty, cement-based paints, tile grout, adhesive, and epoxy coatings.

Since April 25, 2018, Duro Colors Private Limited has been in technical collaboration with Regent Paints Inc., USA, headquartered at 69-44 Cooper Avenue, Glendale, New York, USA.

🌐 Website: www.RegentPaintsUSA.com

Regent Paints Inc. guides Duro Colors Private Limited in formulation, technology upgrades, and marketing. It also authorizes Duro Colors Pvt Ltd to repackage and relabel products under the DUBLIN brand.
